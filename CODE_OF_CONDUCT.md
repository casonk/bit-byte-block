# Code of Conduct

## Expectations

- Be direct, technically rigorous, and respectful.
- Keep operational and security-sensitive details out of public discussion.
- Focus review comments on observable behavior, risks, and maintainability.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or doxxing.
- Publishing secrets, wallet addresses, or local network details without
  approval.
- Requesting unsafe or deceptive changes that hide operational behavior.

## Enforcement

Report serious issues to the maintainers through a private channel rather than a
public issue.
